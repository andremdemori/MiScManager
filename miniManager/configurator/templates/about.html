MiniManager Military Version is a manager/configurator for simulations conducted in Mininet-WiFi, focused on military operations scenarios. Through MiniManager, users can configure both the military scenario and the specifications of the wireless communication network, either by importing the configuration from an ontology or manually.




MiniManager Military Version is an extension of MiniManager. Click the "Download MiniManager Paper" button to download the article about MiniManager.
